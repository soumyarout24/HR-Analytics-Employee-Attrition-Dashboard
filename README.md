# HR Analytics Dashboard – Employee Attrition Insights

## 📌 Project Overview
This project is an interactive HR Analytics Dashboard developed using **Power BI and Excel** to analyze employee attrition trends, workforce demographics, job satisfaction, departmental turnover, and employee retention patterns.

The dashboard helps organizations identify key factors influencing employee attrition and supports HR teams in making data-driven workforce decisions.

---

## 🚀 Key Features
- Interactive HR dashboard with dynamic filtering
- KPI tracking for:
  - Total Employees
  - Attrition Count
  - Attrition Rate
- Employee attrition analysis by:
  - Department
  - Gender
  - Age Group
  - Job Role
  - Education
  - Job Satisfaction
- Workforce demographic insights
- Employee retention analysis
- HR business insights & recommendations
- Interactive visualizations and slicers

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
- **Power BI** – Dashboard development & visualization
- **Excel** – Data cleaning and preprocessing
- **Power Query** – Data transformation
- **DAX** – KPI calculations and measures

---

## 📊 Dashboard Insights

### Key Insights
- Highest attrition observed in the **26–35 age group**
- Research & Development department has the highest attrition
- Male employees show higher attrition compared to females
- Employees with medium job satisfaction levels are more likely to leave
- Laboratory Technicians and Sales Executives have the highest turnover

### Business Recommendations
- Improve employee retention strategies for mid-career employees
- Strengthen employee engagement programs
- Enhance career growth opportunities
- Reduce workload pressure in high-turnover roles
- Improve job satisfaction and work-life balance initiatives
